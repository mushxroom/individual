About

Hui is the abbreviation for Anhui, in the middle east part of China. Hui cuisine is classy by itself. The influence of Hui Cuisine rises and falls with merchants from Anhui, where a number of influencial merchants originated throughout the history of China. It is said that the merchants takes their chefs with them on business trips, and the chefs would cook for their business partners to show respect. Thus, Hui Cuisine bears much of the Hui merchant culture.

Specialties and Features

The Hui region uses ingredients from mountains and farm animals. The chefs focus on the usage of fire extensively to bring out the texture and flavor of different ingredients. Hui Cuisine also adopts a traditional belief that "medicines and food are of the same origin", which means that eating and cooking the right food can enhance health and help people stay away from doctors.
