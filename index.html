Chinese cuisine is famous for the nice flavor and its variety. The cuisine not only soothes your empty stomache, but also conveys stories and tales of the local cities.

China, with such a large territory, the weather and natural conditions vary greatly from province to province, also resulting in different local habits and unique methods of cooking their local specialties. By the end of Qing Dynasty, the Chinese cuisine system finally matured and formed eight mainstream styles with signature dishes shown as follows.
